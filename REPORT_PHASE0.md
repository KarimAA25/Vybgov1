## 2) What was ignored and why

- **Build artifacts** (`build/`, `**/build/`): generated by Flutter builds and safe to regenerate.
- **Dependency folders** (`node_modules/`, `**/node_modules/`): reproducible via package managers.
- **Dart tool directories** (`.dart_tool/`, `**/.dart_tool/`): generated by Flutter/Dart tooling.
- **Archives** (`**/*.zip`): typically snapshots/exports not required for build.
- **Firestore exports** (`Database/*.json`): data exports are not required for local builds.
- **Firebase local artifacts** (`**/.firebase/`): generated by Firebase CLI.

See updated ignore rules in [.gitignore](.gitignore).
